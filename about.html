MicroWorlds JR, for pre-readers, is easy to learn and use with simple, point & click operations and child friendly symbols and icons. The Audio Help feature helps children understand what the icons will do. Generally, MW JR is used from pre-K up to and including Grade 4. It encourages children to explore mathematical ideas such as number, geometry, patterns & movement. It can also be used in science, social studies, geography and history.

MicroWorlds EX is the older sibling of MicroWorlds JR. It is a text-based coding environment, appropriate for upper elementary school through high school students, that serves as a bridge between introductory block-based coding and more advanced coding languages like Java or Python.

Both these coding apps run on Windows 7, 8 and 10 as well as mac OS 10.10 and higher
